About Cat

Cats are popular pets known for their agility and independence. They belong to the Felidae family and come in various breeds with diverse coat colors and patterns. Cats are carnivorous mammals, primarily hunting small prey like rodents. They communicate through body language, purring, and meowing. Their playful nature and affectionate behavior make them cherished companions for many people worldwide.
