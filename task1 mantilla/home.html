Tulip

Tulips, renowned for their vibrant beauty, belong to the lily family and are native to Central Asia. These perennial, bulbous plants are cherished worldwide for their dazzling array of colors, from rich reds and brilliant yellows to soft pinks and pure whites. Tulips have a storied history, with their popularity reaching its zenith during the Dutch Golden Age, sparking the infamous "Tulip Mania" in the 17th century. Today, they symbolize love, elegance, and rebirth, making them a favored choice in gardens and floral arrangements. Their distinct cup-shaped petals and graceful stems continue to captivate garden enthusiasts and flower lovers alike, perpetuating their timeless allure.
